1️⃣ Home Page

Hero Section (top of the page)

Where in HTML: <section id="hero"> or a hero div

Media: Big banner image or video showing your brand, service, or salon

Type: JPG/PNG image or MP4/WebM video

Size hint: Full width, e.g., 1920x1080 px

Why: This is the first thing visitors see — should look attractive and professional

Testimonials Slider

Where in HTML: .testimonial-slider → each .testimonial-item

Media: Client photos or icons

Type: Small JPG/PNG portraits (optional: short video testimonials)

Size hint: 100x100 px (square)

Why: Shows real clients’ faces to build trust

2️⃣ Booking Page

Service Options

Where in HTML: Inside the form, with checkboxes for services

Media: Small icons or images for each service (e.g., haircut, manicure)

Type: PNG/JPG icons

Size hint: 80x80 px

Why: Helps users quickly see what each service is

Staff Selection

Where in HTML: <select id="staffPreference"> dropdown

Media: Staff headshots (optional thumbnail)

Type: PNG/JPG portraits

Size hint: 100x100 px

Why: Shows who will provide the service

Booking Confirmation

Where in HTML: <div id="bookingConfirmation">

Media: Thank you illustration, GIF, or small animation

Type: PNG/GIF/video

Size hint: Flexible; must fit in confirmation box

Why: Gives users visual feedback that booking was successful

3️⃣ Contact Page

Contact Hero / Background

Where in HTML: <section id="contact">

Media: Office photo, background image, or map image

Type: JPG/PNG

Size hint: Full width, e.g., 1920x1080 px

Why: Makes the contact section visually appealing

Form Messages

Where in HTML: <div id="formMessage">

Media: Small icon for success or error (optional)

Type: PNG/SVG

Size hint: 20x20 px

Why: Visually reinforces success/error message

4️⃣ About Page / Team Section (Optional)

Team Photos / Office Images

Where in HTML: Any <section> for team or office

Media: Portraits of team, office, or service images

Type: PNG/JPG (video optional)

Size hint: Portraits: 200x200 px, Banners: 1920x1080 px

Why: Introduces your team and workspace

5️⃣ General Media for All Pages

Logo

Where in HTML: <img class="logo"> in header

Type: PNG (transparent) or SVG

Size hint: 150x50 px

Why: Branding, appears on every page

Favicon

Where in HTML: <link rel="icon" href="favicon.ico">

Type: ICO or PNG

Size hint: 32x32 px

Why: Shows in browser tab

Footer Social Icons

Where in HTML: <footer> → <a><img class="social-icon"></a>

Type: PNG/SVG of social media logos

Size hint: 32x32 px

Why: Links to your social media profiles
