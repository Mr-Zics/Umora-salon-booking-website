About Umora Salon Beauty & Spa

Our story, our passion, our commitment to your beauty and well-being.

Our Journey to Serenity

Umora Salon Beauty & Spa was founded with a vision to create a haven where clients could escape daily stress and indulge in beauty, wellness, and relaxation.

What began as a small local salon has grown into a trusted beauty destination through dedication to excellence, premium products, and personalized care.

Our highly trained professionals stay updated with modern techniques and trends, ensuring every client feels confident, refreshed, and truly pampered.
